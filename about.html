About

Learn more about the app and the creator below. The source code can be found on GitHub. For any issues, suggestions, or general feedback, you can contact me at pixelrazor@gmail.com.

How does it work?

There's not much fancy going on under the hood: We look at all the artists in your playlists, lookup their albums, then add them to a new playlist if they fresh enough!

What are you doing with my account?

Not much; we use the bare minumum permissions needed to read your playlists (both public and private) and to create a new playlist (both public and private).

Who are you and why should I trust you?

I'm a software engineer that takes great pleasure from making things for others. My projects are free to use with no ads; they're purely for your enjoyment! I also keep my code open source for all other to view, so there's no secret behind these curtains!
